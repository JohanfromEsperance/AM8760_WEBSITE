Risk Register Details

Who: Risk managers, safety engineers

What: A register that identifies and assesses risks associated with port assets

When: Continuously, with regular updates

Where: Risk management system

With: Risk assessment tools, databases

How: By identifying, assessing, and documenting risks and mitigation measures

Why: To proactively manage risks and ensure asset safety

Quality Check:

  • Right person (risk managers, engineers)
  • Right time (continuously, updates)
  • Right place (risk management system)
  • Right equipment (assessment tools, databases)
  • Right process (identifying, assessing, documenting)
  • Right work (managing risks)
  • Right reason (ensure safety)

Expanded Activities:

  • Identify potential risks associated with port assets.
  • Assess the likelihood and impact of each risk.
  • Document risks and develop mitigation plans.
  • Regularly update the risk register to reflect new risks or changes in existing risks.

Research and Benchmarking:

  • Best Practices: Follow guidelines from the Risk Management Society (RIMS) for creating risk registers.
  • Case Studies: Review risk registers from other ports to understand common risks and mitigation strategies.
  • Benchmark Data: Compare your risk register with industry standards to ensure comprehensive risk management.

Narrative: This block involves creating and maintaining a risk register that identifies and assesses risks associated with port assets. It includes documenting potential risks, assessing their impact, and implementing mitigation measures to proactively manage risks and ensure the safety of the assets.
