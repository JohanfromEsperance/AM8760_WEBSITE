Contractor Management Process Details

Who: Contract managers, procurement officers

What: Management of external contractors for maintenance and operational tasks

When: Continuously, with periodic reviews

Where: Management and procurement departments

With: Contractor agreements, CMMS

How: By selecting, managing, and monitoring contractors

Why: To ensure quality and compliance with maintenance and operational standards

Quality Check:

  • Right person (contract managers, procurement)
  • Right time (continuously)
  • Right place (management, procurement)
  • Right equipment (software, CMMS)
  • Right process (selecting, managing)
  • Right work (quality and compliance)
  • Right reason (ensure standards)

Expanded Activities:

  • Select and onboard external contractors based on qualifications and performance.
  • Manage contractor agreements and monitor compliance with standards.
  • Use CMMS to track contractor activities and performance.
  • Review and update contractor management processes regularly.

Research and Benchmarking:

  • Best Practices: Follow guidelines from the Project Management Institute (PMI) for contractor management.
  • Case Studies: Review contractor management practices from other ports to identify effective strategies.
  • Benchmark Data: Compare your contractor management practices with industry standards to ensure quality and compliance.
