AM8760-Asset Management Masterclass

The AM8760 Training Manual is a designed to provide asset managers, maintenance professionals, and organisational leaders with the knowledge and tools necessary to implement effective asset management practices.

Based on the guidelines and standards set by ISO 55001 and the GFMAM (Global Forum on Maintenance and Asset Management) Landscape Edition 3, this manual emphasises managing assets over the full 8760 hours in a year.

The training focuses on monetising time at unit rates to express efficiency, covering key areas such as asset value management, compliance, investment strategies, human resource management, and downtime management.

This manual also includes practical workspaces after each section to encourage interactive learning and real-world application. By completing this training, participants will develop a strategic approach to asset management, improve their ability to manage risks and ensure asset reliability, enhance their skills in financial planning and investment decision-making, and foster a culture of continuous improvement and compliance within their organisations.

The AM8760 training program is designed to equip you with a method to "DO" Asset Management by understanding asset management principles and practices.

Emphasising the importance of monetising time at unit rates, this program aims to help you achieve operational efficiency and effectiveness.
