Right Sized Organisation - FTE & Seasonal Uplift Details

Who: Human resources, operations managers

What: Ensuring the organization has the right number of full-time equivalents (FTEs) and seasonal staff

When: Continuously, with periodic reviews

Where: Human resources and operations departments

With: Workforce planning tools, CMMS

How: By analyzing workload and adjusting staff levels accordingly

Why: To ensure the organization has the right staffing levels to meet operational demands

Quality Check:

  • Right person (HR, managers)
  • Right time (continuously)
  • Right place (HR, operations)
  • Right equipment (planning tools, CMMS)
  • Right process (analyzing, adjusting)
  • Right work (right staffing)
  • Right reason (meet demands)

Expanded Activities:

  • Analyze workload and staffing needs based on operational data.
  • Adjust staffing levels to ensure the organization has the right number of FTEs and seasonal staff.
  • Use CMMS to track and manage staff activities.
  • Review and update workforce plans based on changing operational needs.

Research and Benchmarking:

  • Best Practices: Follow guidelines from the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) for workforce planning.
  • Case Studies: Review workforce planning practices from other ports to identify effective strategies.
  • Benchmark Data: Compare your staffing levels and workforce planning practices with industry standards to ensure efficiency.

Narrative: This block involves ensuring the organization has the right number of full-time equivalents (FTEs) and seasonal staff to meet operational demands. This includes analyzing workload, adjusting staffing levels, and managing staff activities to ensure efficiency.
