Planned Maintenance

Definition: Scheduled maintenance activities designed to prevent unexpected failures and extend asset life.

Expanded Description: Planned Maintenance (PM) refers to maintenance tasks that are scheduled in advance based on a predefined plan. These activities are designed to prevent unexpected failures and ensure that assets operate efficiently over their expected lifespan. PM includes routine inspections, servicing, and minor repairs conducted at regular intervals to maintain asset reliability and performance.

Key Components:

  • Maintenance schedules and plans
  • Routine inspections and servicing
  • Preventative maintenance tasks
  • Documentation and record-keeping
  • Performance monitoring and adjustments
