Preparation: AM8760-Game Set-up and Board Layout

You have 45 Minutes to prepare (45Minutes)

1. Review:

  • Training Material
  • The Port of ExSTO Asset Managment System
  • Annual Reports, Policies, SAMP's, AMP, Masterdata
  • Maintenance Plans, Tactics, Startgeies, FMEA's

2. Create an Organisation structure for the Port of ExSTO and appoint team members to roles

3. Review and Anlyse the Port of ExSTO budget and allocate OPEX-CAPEX = TOTEX

4. Determine the Port of ExSTO RUN-RATE (Cost per hour)

5. Review the Port of ExSTO SOA (State of teh Assets) and determine your ongoing asset managmnet strategies

6. Review your Stratgey and Update:

  • The Ports of ExSTO AMP (Asset Management Policy)
  • The Port of ExSTO Maintenance Management Plan
  • The Port of ExSTO Capital Investment Management Plan

7. Select and AVATAR and coloured dice set

8. START the GAME - Start Managing your Assets

Lay the board on a large table and set the crane, avatars and dice to the left hand side at the starting point.

Shuffle and Place the playing cards on the board face down

Every leader get a turn to roll the dice. Once the dice is rolled you have two minutes to make a decision and execute it (Using teh YELLOW sand time to time)

Count the number of block shown on the dice and move your avatar to that position

Pickup the card, read it load for the whole group

Join your Team and Manage your assets using:

  • AM8760 Planning
  • AM8760 Asset Intervention Methodology
  • AM8760 Asset Life-Cycle Strategies
