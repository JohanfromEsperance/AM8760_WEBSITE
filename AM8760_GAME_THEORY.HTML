Evaluating Outcomes: Participants continuously evaluate the outcomes of their decisions and adjust their strategies accordingly. This strategic knowledge helps them learn from their experiences and improve their performance.
Balancing Short-Term and Long-Term Goals: Participants develop strategies for balancing short-term fixes with long-term sustainability. This involves making decisions that address immediate issues while ensuring long-term asset reliability and efficiency.
Executing Strategies: Participants follow the procedures they developed for executing their strategies. This involves implementing step-by-step actions to address the scenarios and achieve their goals.
Problem-Solving Processes: Participants use procedural knowledge to solve problems presented in the scenarios. This includes identifying the issue, analysing information, generating solutions, and implementing the best course of action.

9Key Questions You need to ask:

  • What do we own?
  • Where is it?
  • What does it do?
  • What is it supposed to do?
  • What is its condition?
  • What is its remnant life?
  • What did it cost?
  • What does it cost to keep doing its OEM function?
  • What alternative opportunity do we have?

6W1H - Always answer:

  • WHO is doing it
  • WHAT are they doing
  • WHEN are they doing it
  • WHERE are they doing it
  • WITH what are they doing it
  • WHY are they doing it
  • HOW are they doing it

Check these for both ASSETS and PEOPLE

7R: Verify and do Quality Control:Ask Did the...

  • RIGHT PERSON
  • do the RIGHT TASK
  • at the RIGHT TIME
  • at the RIGHT PLACE
  • using the RIGHT TOOLS and EQUIPMENT
  • and worked to the RIGHT PRECISION / SPECIFICATION
  • for the RIGHT REASON

Check these for both ASSETS and PEOPLE
