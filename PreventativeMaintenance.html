Preventative Maintenance

Definition: Maintenance activities performed regularly to prevent asset failures and maintain optimal performance.

Expanded Description: Preventative Maintenance (PM) involves regular and systematic maintenance activities aimed at preventing asset failures and extending their operational life. PM tasks are performed based on time intervals, usage, or condition criteria, and include activities such as lubrication, adjustments, and component replacements. This proactive approach helps in reducing the likelihood of unexpected breakdowns and ensures consistent asset performance.

Key Components:

  • Maintenance schedules based on time or usage
  • Routine servicing and adjustments
  • Component replacements and upgrades
  • Condition monitoring and diagnostics
  • Documentation and performance tracking
